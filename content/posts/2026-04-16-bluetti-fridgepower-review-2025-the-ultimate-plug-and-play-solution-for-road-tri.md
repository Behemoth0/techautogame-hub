## What Exactly is BLUETTI FridgePower?

At its core, the FridgePower system is a specialized Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) designed for the rigors of the automotive environment. While standard power stations are jacks-of-all-trades, the FridgePower is optimized for the high-surge startup currents required by fridge compressors. 

The "plug-and-play" moniker isn't just marketing fluff. In the past, if you wanted a reliable backup for your Dometic or ARB fridge, you had to tap into your vehicle's wiring, install an isolator, and mount a heavy lead-acid or LiFePO4 battery under the seat or in the trunk. The FridgePower eliminates this. It sits between your vehicle’s 12V/24V outlet and your fridge. When the car is running, it passes power through while simultaneously charging itself. When the engine cuts off, it takes over instantly, ensuring your perishables stay at a crisp 38 degrees Fahrenheit.
