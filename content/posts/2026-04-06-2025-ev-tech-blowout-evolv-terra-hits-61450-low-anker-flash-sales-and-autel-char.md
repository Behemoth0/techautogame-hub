Moving from the road to the home, Anker is currently running a flash sale on its SOLIX E10 backup system. As EV ownership grows, so does the demand for reliable home power. The Anker SOLIX E10 isn't just a portable battery; it’s a sophisticated energy management tool. 

During this flash sale, the E10 (often bundled with solar panels) is seeing price cuts that bring it into the sub-$900 range for the base unit. It features a massive array of ports, including high-wattage USB-C and standard AC outlets, capable of running everything from your high-end gaming rig to a full-sized refrigerator for several hours.
