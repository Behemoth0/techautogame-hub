## The Evolution of ADAS: What to Look For This Year

Before we dive into the deals, it is crucial to understand what makes a 2026 vehicle 'safe.' The industry has moved beyond simple lane-keep assist. We are now seeing 'Level 2+' autonomy as a standard in mid-range trims. When shopping this April, look for these key features:

1. **Intersection Assistance:** Systems that detect cross-traffic and pedestrians when you are making a turn.
2. **Evasive Steering Assist:** Technology that helps the driver navigate around an obstacle if a collision is imminent and braking alone isn't enough.
3. **Driver Monitoring Systems (DMS):** Using infrared cameras to ensure the driver is attentive, a feature now heavily weighted in 2026 safety ratings.
